ABOUT

The Panchakki water mill is helmed important as it was initally used for grinding grains. for visiting pilgrims. Also considered a monument of importance of the Aurangabad region, the water mill in Maharashtra is considered a scientific brilliance. It is said to be an amalgamation of India architecture pretaining to the Middle Ages and scientific know-how. The Panchakki Water Mill was originally conceptulisation and erected to generate renewable energy by harnessing it from running water supplied by nearby source of water,like a spring flowing down the mountain slopes.

Best time to visit

The water mill naturally comes alive during the monsoon season as the monsoons recieve heavy rainfall. The winter months between November abd February can get chilly. However, most tourists seem to enjoy the winter bliss!
